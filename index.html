Mortal

The owner and the leader of the team, Naman Mathur aka MortaL is widely regarded as one of the best PUBGM players. He also has a huge fanbase, with 5M subscribers on YT, the highest any Indian PUBGM Youtuber has. He also reached #3 rank in Asia. He is often known for his postive attitude towards everyone and his high IQ and gamesense.

VipeR

Often regarded as the two thumb king in India, Yash Soni aka Viper is known for his friendship with MortaL. He is beast when comes to frags. He plays as a Support Fragger for the team.

Regaltos

Known as one of the best assaulters in India, Parv Singh aka Regaltos aka Regachu is the entry fragger for Team SouL. His thirst for kills in the game has made him MVP in the game many times when SouL has won matches in national and international levels.

Aman

Aman Jain is widely regarded as one of the most aggressive players in the country. During one of bad times when SouL wasn't performing well, Aman stepped up as the IGL of team for a time. The team performed exceptionally well under his leadership. He was the MVP of PMCO Fall 2019 SA region with Entity Gaming.

Sangwan

Dhruv Sangwan is the current IGL of team SouL. He joined Team SouL in early 2020. He adapted well with the team and proved himself with a string of good individual performances which includes MVP in 2nd game of PMPL SA league stage
